# Privacy Policy for White Noise: Sleep & Baby

**Last updated:** March 1, 2026

## Overview

White Noise: Sleep & Baby respects your privacy. This app is designed to provide relaxing white noise and sleep sounds without collecting personal information.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

---

## Information We Do Not Collect

We do **not** collect:

- Names
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
- Usage analytics
- Audio recordings
- Contacts or photos
- Any personally identifiable information

The app works fully offline and does not require account creation.

---

## In-App Purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases processed securely through Apple’s App Store.

We do not process payments directly and do not store payment information.

Purchase validation is handled by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not store any personal data on our own servers.

For more information about how Apple handles purchase data, please review Apple’s Privacy Policy:  
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy is available at:  
https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

---

## Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat solely to manage in-app purchase functionality.

We do not use:
- Advertising SDKs
- Tracking technologies
- Analytics services
- External data collection tools

---

## Data Storage

All sound settings and preferences are stored locally on your device.

We do not operate servers and do not store user data externally.

---

## Children’s Privacy

This app is designed for a general audience and may be used by parents for baby sleep sounds.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Since no personal data is collected, the app complies with applicable children's privacy regulations.

---

## Your Privacy Rights

Because we do not collect or store personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
